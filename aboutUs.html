On a mission

To be the best-loved event ticketing platform in the world.

Since Ticket Tailor was launched back in 2010, we’ve always built our platform around what event organisers really want: stellar service, features and support – without the sky-high prices.

We measure ourselves by the reviews (and love) we get from the event organisers who use us, the speed and quality of our support and the reliability of our product and releases.

We’re all about you

Our aim is, and has always been, to create a ticketing platform that puts event organisers first.

100% independent

We have no investors and we’re proud of it. We create value for event organisers.

Always growing

We balance growing our business with the wider good. That means fair pricing for all.
